WHAT IS BOTTLE ART ?

Bottle art is a method of drawing and decorating over glass bottles ,which ususaly contains a theme and a message to convey ,Bottle arts are used for decorations and can also be used as lamp by inserting light in it .Sillout effect using lamp is also a part of bottle art.

HOW BOTTLE ARTS ARE MADE ?

Creating bottle art consist of various process like collection of bottles ,slecttion of theme and art ,prievew of art ,base coating,drawing art using hand ,heat setting,varnish coating.
